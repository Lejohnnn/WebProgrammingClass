About Me

Greetings, my name is Lejohn Thomas; I am an 18-year-old information technology student at Florida A&M Univeristy. I was born and raised in West Palm Beach, Florida, and I am the youngest of four and an only child simultaneously. I am of Caribbean descent, specifically Jamaican, and I take pride in that.

Since I was a kid, I’ve always enjoyed cooking and hoped to one day in the future attend culinary school. It’s now become a hobby I enormously enjoy. I’m also very into listening to music; I enjoy various genres and listen to music practically all day. I tend to listen to hip-hop and R&B music throughout the day. I’m heavily involved in robotics and other “nerdy” activities. I participated in robotics since 6th grade and loved it. I’ve also been programming for a few years now and love it, hence my choice of major. I usually hang out with my friends when I’m not doing any of these things.

I plan to become a software developer for a large tech company. I also plan on returning to college to pursue a master’s degree. As I leave college, I hope to travel more and leave the country more often. With that, I plan to move out of Florida and settle in a big city in the US.

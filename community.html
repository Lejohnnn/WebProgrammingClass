While attending Florida Agricultural and Mechanical University I have involved myself in various clubs, within those clubs I have participated in many different volunteering activities. Recently I have involved myself with an organization called the Black Data Processing Association. With this organization I volunteered at The Kearney Center, here I assisted with the preparation and serving of meals to the homeless.

I really enjoyed this experience and was able to meet some amusing individuals, it re-sparked my interest in giving back to my local community. I plan to come back on my own time next semester to build a relationship with the people and the organization.

With the Black Data Processing Association, I also volunteered at the Lawrence Gregory Community center. Here I was able to work with local youth, I enjoy working with kids so this volunteering event peaked my interest. Here I assisted students with homework and played various field games while building connections with each individual. Next semester I plan to volunteer often with this community center so I can strengthen my connection with the individuals I met and mentor them throughout their life.
